Our Services

Residential Junk Removal

We clear out homes, garages, and yards, ensuring your living space stays clutter-free. Our team handles everything from old furniture to appliances and general household junk.

Commercial Cleanouts

Efficient removal of office furniture, equipment, and debris for businesses of all sizes. We minimize disruption to your operations while ensuring a clean, junk-free workspace.

Eco-Friendly Disposal

We prioritize recycling and responsible disposal to minimize environmental impact. Our team sorts materials and ensures that as much as possible is recycled or donated.

Large Item Pickup

Specializing in the removal of bulky items like furniture, appliances, and electronics. We have the equipment and expertise to safely remove and dispose of oversized items.

Dumpster Rental

Convenient dumpster rentals for DIY projects and large-scale cleanouts. We offer various sizes to suit your needs and provide prompt delivery and pickup.

Yard Waste Removal

Efficient removal of leaves, branches, and other yard debris. We help maintain your outdoor spaces by removing organic waste and ensuring proper disposal or composting.

About EcoJunk

EcoJunk is your trusted partner for efficient, eco-friendly junk removal services. We're committed to keeping your space clean while protecting the environment.
