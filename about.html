About EcoJunk

Turning Trash into Treasure, One Pickup at a Time

Revolutionizing Junk Removal with a Green Touch

At EcoJunk, we're more than just a junk removal company. We're a team of passionate individuals committed to making a positive impact on our environment and communities. Our eco-friendly approach to junk removal sets us apart, ensuring that we minimize waste and maximize recycling and repurposing efforts.

Our Story

From Small Beginnings to Big Impact

Founded in 2010 by environmental enthusiast John Doe, EcoJunk started as a small family business with a single truck and a big dream. John's passion for cleaner, greener spaces drove him to create a junk removal service that prioritized eco-friendly practices.

Over the years, we've grown from a local operation to a trusted name in junk removal across the region. Our commitment to sustainability and customer satisfaction has remained unwavering, allowing us to make a significant impact on both our community and the environment.

Today, EcoJunk continues to lead the way in sustainable junk removal, constantly innovating and finding new ways to reduce waste and promote recycling. We're proud of how far we've come and excited about the positive change we continue to make every day.

Our Mission & Values
